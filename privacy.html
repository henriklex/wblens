Privacy Policy for WB Lens

Last updated: April 2026

What data WB Lens collects

WB Lens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no crash reporting service, no advertising SDK, and no backend server.

Camera access

WB Lens requests access to your device camera solely to capture photos of whiteboards for scanning. Camera frames are processed on-device in real time to detect whiteboard edges. No camera data is transmitted off your device.

Photo library access

WB Lens may request access to your photo library if you choose to import an existing photo. Photos are accessed only when you explicitly select them. No photos are transmitted off your device.

Image processing

All image processing (perspective correction, enhancement) happens entirely on your device using Apple's Core Image framework. Processed images are stored only in the app's local library on your device.

Sharing

When you use the share function, you choose where to send your scanned image. WB Lens passes the file to iOS's standard share sheet — the app has no involvement in or knowledge of where you send it.

Children

WB Lens does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.

Contact

For any questions, please open an issue at github.com/henriklex/wblens.
